About Our Team

When you first use the Cleaning House mobile application, you will be asked to choose a role. The available roles are Cleaner and Host. Cleaners are people who wish to earn money by cleaning properties posted on the application. Hosts are property owners who are willing to pay a Cleaner to clean one or more of their properties. Once you have chosen your role, the application will remember your choice in the future.
